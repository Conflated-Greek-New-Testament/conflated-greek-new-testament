The Conflated Greek New Testament in English

A public-domain translation synthesizing the Greek manuscript tradition

About This Project

This translation seeks to present the New Testament in English by synthesizing the full range of extant Greek manuscript evidence, including both the Critical Text tradition and the Textus Receptus / Byzantine tradition.

Rather than privileging a single textual stream, this work preserves meaningful variants transparently within the text while maintaining clarity, readability, and fidelity to the Greek.

Initial Release

The Gospel according to Matthew is presented here as the first stage of an ongoing project to translate the entire New Testament — and potentially the whole Bible — using this conflated-eclectic method.
